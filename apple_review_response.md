Hello App Review Team,

Thank you for your review. Below is the requested information about our business model and the update we made for the Diet section.

1. Who are the users that will use the paid content in the app?
   The users are clients and patients of fitness coaches and nutrition professionals who work with them directly outside the app.

2. Where can users purchase the content that can be accessed in the app?
   They do not purchase digital content inside the app. Any payment is for the human coaching or nutrition service provided directly by the professional outside the iOS app, using the professional's own billing channel.

3. What specific types of previously purchased content can a user access in the app?
   Users can access the care plans that were personally assigned to them by their coach or nutrition professional, including personalized diet plans and workout plans.

4. What paid content, subscriptions, or features are unlocked within the app that do not use In-App Purchase?
   None. The iOS app does not sell, unlock, or gate digital content, subscriptions, or premium features. It is a client companion app that displays the plans assigned by the user's professional.

5. How do users obtain an account? Do users have to pay a fee to create an account?
   Accounts are created by the professional for their clients. Clients do not pay a fee to create an account inside the app, and there is no account purchase flow in the iOS app.

6. Can users purchase physical goods or services together with digital content in your app?
   No. Users cannot purchase physical goods or digital goods inside the app.

Regarding Guideline 1.4.1:
We updated the Diet section so that source citations are now visible directly in the diet plan view. The citations are easy to find in a dedicated "Fuentes del plan" section above the meal list, and each citation opens the corresponding external source link. We also remind users to consult a doctor before making medical decisions.

The review account used for App Review now shows these citations in the Diet section.

Please let us know if any additional information is needed.
